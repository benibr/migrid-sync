About IDMC

IDMC or Imaging Data Management Center at University of Copenhagen (KU/UCPH) is meant for storing, sharing, previewing and processing especially imaging research data. The intended audience is employees, their collaboration partners and students. IDMC delivers safe central storage space for own and shared (image) files in addition to previewing and processing image data. You can use IDMC as a secure network drive from anywhere and it also comes with interactive analysis tools.
IDMC is delivered by SCIENCE HPC Center and is formally for the faculty of Science, but others have access especially for collaboration purposes. A roll-out to all of UCPH might be underway to support the general UCPH Data Management Guidelines.

Sensitive and Personal Data (GDPR)

Please note that while IDMC comes with a strong security focus, it is only a solution approved for general scientific data and not for highly sensitive data. In particular it is not for personal data classified as sensitive in the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In case you need to work on such sensitive data we recommend the IDMC sister facility SIF, which is intended and approved for exactly that purpose.
